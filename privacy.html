Romblon State University (RSU) respects your right to privacy and is committed to protecting the confidentiality of your personal information. The University has adopted reasonable administrative, physical, and technical measures to prevent the loss, misuse, or alteration of information under our control. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

By filling out and submitting information through this system, you consent to the collection, processing, and use of your personal information in accordance with this Privacy Notice and the provisions of the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (R.A. 10173).

Personal Information Collected
  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Mobile/phone number
  • Course, major, and year level
  • Student ID number
  • Other details required for queueing, monitoring, and registrar-related services
Purpose of Collection
  • Access provision to RSU REQS
  • Attendance and transaction monitoring
  • Evaluation and documentation
  • Communication and notifications
  • Queueing and scheduling services for registrar-related processes

All information you provide is collected and stored securely. In some cases, data may be processed through third-party services (e.g., Google), which have their own privacy policies. See: Google Privacy Policy .

RSU shall retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes stated above, after which it shall be securely disposed of.

For concerns or inquiries regarding data privacy, you may contact the University Data Protection Officer at dpo@rsu.edu.ph. The Office of Quality Assurance ensures that your personal data is respected and protected in compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (R.A. 10173).
